Full-text fuzzy search across items (typo-tolerant), mirroring Python's search.

Returns SearchResult entries (matched item + relevance score + matchedIn) ordered by relevance. contextFilter restricts to a kref prefix; kindFilter is an exact kind match; includeRevisionMetadata / includeArtifactMetadata widen the search; minScore filters by relevance (0.0-1.0); pageSize/cursor paginate.
