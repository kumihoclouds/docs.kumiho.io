Kumiho C++ SDK
==============

**Graph-native creative & AI asset management for C++**

Kumiho is a C++ SDK for `Kumiho Cloud <https://kumiho.io>`_, a graph-native
creative and AI asset management platform designed for VFX, animation, and
AI-driven workflows.
